About

I am a PhD candidate in Physics at Caltech working with Professor Nick Hutzler. My research focuses on developing quantum sensing protocols for fundamental physics, particularly in the context of precision measurements and symmetry violation searches.

During my PhD, I have worked on several projects including engineering molecular clock transitions that are insensitive to environmental noise while maintaining high sensitivity to physics beyond the Standard Model. This work has applications in searching for new sources of CP violation and understanding the matter-antimatter asymmetry of the universe.
